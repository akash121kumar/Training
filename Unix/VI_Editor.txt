VI Editor


i- 	Insert at the cursor(goes into insert mode)
a- 	Write after cursor(goes into insert mode)
A- 	Write at the end of the line(goes into insert mode)
Esc- 	Terminate insert mode
u- 	Undo last change
U- 	Undo all changes to the entire line
o- 	opens a new line(goes into insert mode)
dd- 	Delete a line
3dd- 	Delete 3 lines
D- 	Delete contents of a line after a cursor
c- 	Deletes contents of a line after the cursor and insert new text
dw-	Delete word
4dw- 	Deletes 4 words
cw-  	Change word
x- 	Delete character at the cursor
r- 	Replace character
R-	Overwrite characters from cursor
s- 	Substitute one character under cursor
S- 	Substitute entire line and begin enter
~-	Change case of individual character


MOVING WITHIN A FILE:
k - 	Move cursor up
j -	Move cursor down
h - 	move cursor left
l -	move cursor right
